DUAL HYD G+Y SYS LO PR — Logical Sequence (how it really runs)

⏱ TIME = runs on the clock (only the failure). 🔗 COND = runs on a trigger condition (crew state) — not a timer. ∥ = parallel calls that fire when their condition is met, as in real life.

  1. ⏱ T+4sstructural_fail — turbine blade release; GREEN AND YELLOW pressure ≤ 1 450 PSI. HYD G+Y SYS LO PR (Level 3 red, CRC + MASTER WARN) — full ECAM appears at once (PTU OFF · G ENG1 PUMP OFF · Y ENG2 PUMP OFF · Y ELEC PUMP ON · MANEUVER WITH CARE · LAND ASAP).
  2. 🔗 AVIATEFly first — F/CTL has reverted to ALTERNATE LAW (PROT LOST), AP 1+2 INOP. PF: wings level, smooth inputs, MAX 320 KT / M.77. PM cancels MASTER WARN.
  3. 🔗 ECAMECAM drill (in FCOM order): PTU OFF → G ENG1 PUMP OFF → Y ENG2 PUMP OFF → Y ELEC PUMP ON (charges accumulator). +3 s later the SECONDARY FAILURES appear (* F/CTL · * WHEEL) + MASTER CAUT.
  4. 🔗 STATUSSTATUS page — STOP ECAM → OEB/RESET → PM reads STATUS aloud (MAX 320/M.77 · FLAP 3 · VREF+25 · L/G GRVTY EXTN · BRK Y ACCU) → ECAM ACTIONS COMPLETED.
  5. 🔗 PLANDecide & communicate — LDG PERF → FORDEC (divert VABB RWY 27, 3 445 m) ∥ after FORDEC: INFORM ATC + FMGC PREP + NITS BRIEF run together → PA → company → emergency services.
  6. 🔗 APPROACHILS RWY 27, hand-flown (AP INOP) — stabilise at VAPP CONF 3 FIRST, THEN L/G GRAVITY EXTN → at L/G DN F/CTL goes DIRECT LAW. Accumulator brakes (~7), no anti-skid, no reverse, long rollout.
  7. 🔗 STOPPEDAfter landing — no NW steering; request tow / follow-me to clear runway.

Situation Brief

Situation: Cruise FL350, VIDP–VABB. A turbine blade release has damaged both GREEN and YELLOW hydraulic return lines simultaneously — system pressure on both circuits drops below 1 450 PSI. HYD G+Y SYS LO PR warning fires (Level 3, red, CRC). Both engines and AC buses remain normal — BLUE system continues on its ELEC pump (RAT NOT deployed for pure G+Y loss). F/CTL reverts to ALTERNATE LAW (PROT LOST); STABILIZER, SPOILERS 1/2/4/5, FLAPS, YAW DAMPER, ANTI SKID and NW STEERING all INOP.

Job: Fly the aircraft first — alternate law, MANEUVER WITH CARE, MAX 320 KT / M.77. Run the HYD G+Y SYS LO PR ECAM per FCOM PRO-ABN-HYD: PTU OFF → AFFECTED PUMPS OFF → YELLOW ELEC PUMP ON (if Y lost by ENG 2 PUMP LO PR). PM declares MAYDAY to ATC once aircraft under control. Land FLAP 3 at VREF+25 kt; gravity gear extension AFTER stabilised at VAPP (so trim reference is set before direct law). Divert to nearest suitable airport with adequate runway.
