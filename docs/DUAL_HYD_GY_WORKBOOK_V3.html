Situation Brief

Situation: Cruise FL350, VIDP–VABB. A turbine blade release damages both GREEN and YELLOW hydraulic return lines simultaneously. Pressure on both circuits drops below 1 450 PSI. HYD G+Y SYS LO PR warning fires (Level 3, red, CRC). Both engines and AC buses remain normal — BLUE system continues on its ELEC pump. F/CTL reverts to ALTERNATE LAW (PROT LOST); STABILIZER, SPOILERS 1/2/4/5, FLAPS, YAW DAMPER, ANTI SKID and NW STEERING all INOP.

Job: Fly first — alternate law, MANEUVER WITH CARE, MAX 320 KT / M.77. Run the HYD G+Y SYS LO PR ECAM: PTU OFF → AFFECTED PUMPS OFF → YELLOW ELEC PUMP ON. Declare MAYDAY. Land FLAP 3 at VREF+25 kt. Gravity gear AFTER stabilised at VAPP. Divert to VABB RWY 27.
