Situation Brief
Situation: Cruise FL350. Sudden loud bang — structural failure. CABIN ALT HI warning fires. Cabin altitude rising above 14,000 ft. TUC at FL350 without oxygen: 15–30 seconds.

Job: Crew dons O2 masks IMMEDIATELY. Announce to passengers. Initiate emergency descent to FL100. Squawk 7700. Declare MAYDAY. Plan diversion.
