Situation Brief

Situation: Departing runway 28 at New Delhi Indira Gandhi (VIDP). Two seconds after passing V1 at 145 kt, a MASTER WARNING fires and the ECAM displays ENG 1 FIRE. Climbing through approximately 400 ft AGL with gear retracting.

Job: PF — Aviate: maintain V2+10, engage AP1, command ECAM ACTIONS. PM — Execute ECAM procedure in order. Both — LAND ASAP decision.
